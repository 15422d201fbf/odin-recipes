Braised sea bream

Description

The braised sea bream is a healthy and nutritious meal rich in protein and other good vitamins. This recipe is cost effective, easy and delicious to make, perfect for family dinners or special events.

Ingredients (for 2)

  • 1 average sized sea-bream, scaled and gutted (500g)
  • 1 citrus
  • 1 bunch of thyme, rosemary and bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of paprika
  • 1 piece of baking-proof cloth
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180C.
  2. Get your fish out of the fridge, score both sides equally with a sharp knife, reserve. (like a chess board)
  3. Cut the citrus into slices and put them into the gut.
  4. Wrap the thyme, rosemary and bay leaves into the cloth, put inside the gut.
  5. Pat the fish dry with a clean towel, then pour 1tbsp of olive oil on each side.
  6. Season each side equally with paprika, salt and pepper to your liking.
  7. Once the oven is heated, cook the fish on a grill for 12 minutes on each side. You can optionally brush the fish with olive oil if it gets too dry.
  8. Pour some citrus juice on the plate and enjoy while it's hot.
